User Consent for AI Apps on Google Storage

Committed to Your Privacy and Explicit Consent
By using the service through in-app purchases, your privacy remains our top priority while translating documents. We ensure transparency and control over how your data is used.

  • Data Sharing with Neural Machine Translation (NMT) Models: The content of your documents is transmitted to Google Storage and Google's NMT models for translation purposes only.

Once translation is complete, the document will be deleted from the NMT models.
