SolvePDF Privacy Policy

SolvePDF is an AI-powered PDF problem-solving app providing both free and premium (subscription) services. This privacy policy explains how SolvePDF collects, uses, and manages personal information. By using the app, you agree to this policy. Collected data is used solely for providing and improving our services.

Information Collected and Used

SolvePDF may collect the following personal information:

  • Email address and profile name for user identification
  • In-app purchase and subscription status information
  • Log data (IP address, device details, etc.) for error reporting and service improvement

Purpose of Collecting Personal Information

The collected information is used for:

  • User account creation and management
  • Providing services and processing in-app purchases
  • Analyzing and diagnosing service improvements

Data Storage and Security

We follow industry-standard security procedures to protect your personal information. However, no method of internet transmission or storage can guarantee absolute security. Selected PDF documents are not stored without user consent and are temporarily used only during the problem-solving process.

Third-Party Service Providers

SolvePDF may use third-party services for providing and improving services, including:

  • AI providers (e.g., OpenAI, Google AI) for problem-solving and translation
  • Cloud storage providers (e.g., Google Cloud Storage)

These third parties adhere strictly to data privacy agreements and do not use personal information for other purposes.

Advertising Information

Ads may be provided to free users, offering additional benefits such as extra usage. Personal identifiable information is not collected during ad delivery.
